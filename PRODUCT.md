# TasteBud — Product Context

## Product Purpose
QR-code-based table ordering system for hospitality venues. Guests scan a QR code at their table, browse the menu, customise items, and pay via Stripe — without waiting for staff. Venues get orders directly.

## Register
product

## Users
- **Primary:** Dine-in guests (18–45), on their phone, usually in ambient light or dimly lit venues. One hand free. Slightly impatient. Expects the experience to feel as good as the venue itself.
- **Secondary:** Venue operators (admin panel). Manage menu, view orders.

## Brand
- Name: TasteBud
- Brand colour: #F9BA0B (gold/amber)
- Accent: black (#111)
- Tone: warm, confident, frictionless. Never corporate. Feels like it belongs in a good pub or restaurant.
- Anti-references: generic SaaS, clinical white, cold blue fintech

## Strategic Principles
- Speed over everything. Guest should be at checkout in under 60 seconds.
- Every screen earns its place. No decorative flows.
- Mobile-only. Desktop is irrelevant for the guest experience.
- Delight without distraction. Small moments of polish; never loud.
